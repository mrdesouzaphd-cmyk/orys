## Theoretical lenses (Vol. 1, Part II)

### Lens 1 — Meaningful Learning
**Ausubel, D. P.** (1968). *Educational Psychology: A Cognitive View.* Holt, Rinehart and Winston.
> Anchor for prior knowledge as the foundation of meaningful new learning. Used in Layer 1 (Activation) and the Conceptual knowledge dimension.

### Lens 2 — Emotional Intelligence
**Goleman, D.** (1995). *Emotional Intelligence: Why It Can Matter More Than IQ.* Bantam Books.
> Anchor for emotional regulation in learning. Used in Layer 3 (Orys Pattern™ Intervention) and Big Five soft skill mapping.

### Lens 3 — Growth Mindset
**Dweck, C.** (2006). *Mindset: The New Psychology of Success.* Random House.
> Anchor for tolerance of error and productive discomfort. Used in Layer 4 (Quick Evidence) and Patterns 4 + 6.

### Lens 4 — Core Skills for the Future of Work
**OECD** (2018). *The Future of Education and Skills 2030.*
**World Economic Forum** (2020). *The Future of Jobs Report.*
**UNESCO** (2021). *Reimagining Our Futures Together.*
> Anchor for professional competencies the lesson develops. Used in the Final Synthesis and Graduate-profile indicator.

## Pedagogical frameworks (taxonomic)

**Bloom, B. S.** (1956). *Taxonomy of Educational Objectives: Cognitive Domain.* Longman.

**Anderson, L. W., & Krathwohl, D. R.** (2001). *A Taxonomy for Learning, Teaching, and Assessing: A Revision of Bloom's Taxonomy of Educational Objectives.* Longman.
> Used for: Bloom verb adjustment, 4 knowledge dimensions (Factual / Conceptual / Procedural / Metacognitive), Layer 2 (Strategies for the Verb).

**Biggs, J.** (2003). *Teaching for Quality Learning at University: What the Student Does.* Open University Press.
> Used for: Constructive Alignment principle — objective → activity → assessment coherence.

## Adult learning & andragogy

> Used for: andragogical profile (target audience 18+), self-directed learning, problem-centered orientation.

```
*Theoretical grounding: [2–4 most relevant authors for this layer/pattern].*
```

For institutional / accreditation reports, the full citation chain should be appended as a footer.
